Profiles

Here are the profiles of some of the cutest pooches you will ever find:

Name: Teddy
Age: 2 years
Gender: male
Breed: Labrador

Teddy is my dog. Teddy is my buddy. Teddy is my life. I love him with all my being. He loves scratching, sniffing and chasing and is the best partner in crime for all his friends. Yet he would love to make more friends his own age as he is outgrowing the puppies in the park.

Name: Niko
Age: 6 months
Gender: female
Breed: Bearded Collie
Favourite Activity: Playing Fetch

Niko is the most energetic furry friend you could have. He has a shiny fur and he likes to jump on sofas when no one is looking. His favourite play is tug-of-war and he especially enjoys going to new places by car. He is very friendly and loves playing with other dogs, so if you would like to meet him for a shot at fun time, please arrange a playdate.

Name: Bruno
Age: 5 years
Gender: male
Breed: Cocker Spaniel

Bruno is an amazing dog. He is the cutest and the kindest being I could hope for. He loves hanging out with other dogs and we would be forever greatful if we could find a playdate for our beloved Bruno. Please contact us to arrange a playdate with Bruno.
